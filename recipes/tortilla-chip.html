Tortilla chip

Description

This recipe will show you how to make a SINGULAR tortilla chip. Please do not ask how to make multiple chips; the technology is not quite there yet to produce en masse and the last time someone attempted to utilize this recipe for multiple chips, a 10 mile sinkhole was created and the land is now uninhabitable for the next 600 years.

Ingredients:

  • corn, or as the native americans called it, maize
  • rock or something
  • salt
  • donkey
  • two bottles of tequila (with optional worm)
  • tomato (NOT OPTIONAL!!)

Steps

  1. find a cow and ask him to point you in the direction of a corn field (you may ask the same cow that assisted you with the burger recipe)
  2. throw salt at the cow to remind him that you're coming for him soon
  3. journey thru the valleys and desert; drink the tequila to give you liquid courage for when the little chihuahuas start barking at you
  4. Use the rock to bash open the padlock on the back of the cornfield's fence
  5. C O N S U M E
