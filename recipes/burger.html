Burger

Description

This recipe will show you how to create an exotic food called a "burger". This food is made by combining raw beef with several exotic spices. Oh yeah, some bread is involved.

Ingredients:

  • cow
  • bread
  • salt
  • pepper
  • lettuce
  • tomato (NOT OPTIONAL!!)

Steps

  1. find a cow and ask him for a hunk of beef
  2. add salt and pepper to beef
  3. I guess you can cook it
  4. grab the bread and place it on top AND below the beef
  5. grab the lettuce and tomato and slap it onto that bad boy
