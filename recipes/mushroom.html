Mushrooms

Description

This recipe will show you how to overcome the negativity from your friends when you tell them that you like mushrooms. Like seriously though, how could you not like them?!?

Ingredients:

  • Article on health benefits of mushrooms
  • Fist
  • 2-3 jokes about your friends outfits
  • tomato (NOT OPTIONAL!!)

Steps

  1. Order a sandwich or burger with mushrooms on it
  2. Use your fist to assault the first friend who makes a comment about how mushrooms are gross
  3. Insult the outfit of the next friend who makes a negative comment about mushrooms (you may compare their outfit to the pattern on the cow you encountered for the burger recipe)
  4. Chuckle while you contemplate how funny it is the way Shitake mushrooms are spelled
